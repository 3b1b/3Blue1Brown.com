## About

Bridge to Enter Advanced Mathematics (BEAM) creates pathways for students historically excluded from STEM opportunities to become scientists, engineers, mathematicians, and innovators. Through rigorous math enrichment, mentorship, and long-term support, BEAM helps students access advanced academic programs, thrive in top high schools and colleges, and pursue STEM careers.

Our approach is holistic, combining deep mathematical learning with advising, community, and sustained guidance from middle school through college. By investing early and consistently, we ensure students not only gain access, but also the tools and confidence to succeed. BEAM is committed to making high-quality STEM education accessible regardless of financial circumstances.
