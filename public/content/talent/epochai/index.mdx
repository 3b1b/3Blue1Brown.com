## About

AI math capabilities are advancing faster than ever. To truly test the frontier, we need problems that haven't been "solved" by the internet. We are building a collection of original, research-level mathematics problems that have resisted serious attempts by professional mathematicians. 

By contributing a problem, you help create a yardstick for machine intelligence—and if an AI solves it, you've facilitated a meaningful advance in human knowledge.

## The Challenge

What makes a FrontierMath problem? We are looking for submissions that satisfy these requirements:

* **Open:** No solution is known.
* **Hard:** At least two professional mathematicians have tried to solve it.
* **Interesting:** A solution would be worthy of publication in at least a standard specialty journal and would be at least somewhat likely to generate further new mathematics.
* **Verifiable:** Solutions can be verified to a high degree of confidence by a typical computer program (not Lean) running on a typical laptop in under an hour.
* **Solvable:** A verifiable solution should be likely to exist.
